Admission Procedure - B.Tech Programme
TheEAPCET - Engineering, Agriculture and Pharmacy Common Entrance Testconvener fills up 70% (Category - A) of the seats  by allotting the candidates to this institute on the basis of their choice, their EAPCET rank and the reservation policy of the Govt. of Andhra Pradesh.The remaining 30% (Category - B) of the seats are filled up by the Institute under the Management /NRI quota as per the guidelines given by the APSCHE and the Govt. of Andhra Pradesh for this purpose.
The convener of ECET admits 10% of the sanctioned intake for each program for admission directly into the II year of the 4-year B.Tech program from among the Diploma holders, based on their rank in ECET, their choice of the institute and the reservation policy of the Govt. of Andhra Pradesh, under the lateral entry scheme.The following is the  sanctioned intake  as per AICTE file No: F.No. South-Central/1-43656147326/2024/EOA/Corrigendum-1 Date: 22-May-2024

M.Tech - Eligibility Requirements:
A bachelor's degree in Engg/Technology  of recognized University in the concerned discipline.
AMIE/AMIETE as equivalent to B.Tech. valid GATE Score obtained in the GATE Examination. Apart from requirements of qualifications as stated above, the admissions of sponsored candidates shall be taken.
